HOW

It's very easy to get your pictures into the "DAMN FINE KEYBOARDS". put your keyboards in the right light and take pretty pictures. To make sure that the photos are yours, I need two photos. one of the photo that should be in the DAMN FINE KEYBOARDS and one of the keyboard with a piece of paper with a random number sequence. In the email you write me the following information:

- Your name to be published
- Name of the keyboard
- Name of the switches
- Name of the Keycaps
- Mods you have done

send the whole thing to:
info@damnfinekeyboards.com


You can also contact me if you want to be part of the DAMN FINE KEYBOARDS. the content is not limited to photos but serves as a basis for many possibilities - articles, art, poems - do what you want. The only requirement is that it is not commercial or pure advertising.
